Science and Technology

Main Features

Motherboard

A motherboard is the main printed circuit board (PCB) in general-purpose computers and other expandable systems.

Laptop

Laptops are generally smaller in size than desktop computers and are battery powered.

Mobile Phone

A form of technology-mediated communication that enables the user of a mobile device to communicate with someone in a different location.

Drone

an uncrewed aircraft or vessel guided by remote control or onboard computers.
